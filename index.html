Thanks for signing in! Here's a quick tour to show you how to get the most out of this site.

Canned Responses

This is the main section for your responses. The left column lets you **add new responses** and **search** through your existing ones.

Add New Responses

Click the **Add New Response** button to create a new response. You can give it a label and assign it to a category.

Search

Use the search bar to find responses quickly by their label or content.

Categories

Organize your responses with custom categories. You can create new categories with the **** button.

Manage Categories

Click the vertical ellipsis () next to a category to **rename** it, **change its color**, or **delete** it.

Dynamic Placeholders

Many of the default responses contain special placeholders like `[Customer's Name]` or `[TrackingNumber]`.

How it Works

When you click **Copy** on a response with placeholders, a small window will pop up asking for the values. Fill them out, click copy again, and the final response will be ready to paste!
